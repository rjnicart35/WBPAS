About Us

The Web-based Program Assessment System is designed to give you an understanding of your specialization and the career areas that you would be best suited to. It takes less than 5 minutes! We consider this quiz as the first step of a thorough understanding and approach for continuous program level improvement. This assessment system has the ultimate goal to clearly identify the programs educational goals and possible outcomes that students are expected to demonstrate and achieve as a result.

After formulating the results of the assessment:

  • You will be able to see your preferred course
  • This includes course overview and more information
  • Career opportunities and expectations, employment outlooks

For students, making proper choices is essential. According to Malubay et. al (2015), choosing a program in college is a complicated decision for students since it determines the kind of vocation they intend to pursue in life. Majority of the problems that the incoming first year students are facing today is what program they would like to take up in tertiary education, and the researchers believe that it is all up to their choices and decision itself. In that case, the researchers aim to develop a web-based program assessment system with a function that allows students to take an assessment to be able to analyze students` choice in taking Technology Education programs. The result for this study will be attainable through an assessment which focuses on assessing what and how an academic program is contributing to the learning and development of students. The goal of this academic program assessment is to analyze students` choice in taking Technology Education for continuous program level improvement. With the ultimate goal to clearly identify the programs` educational goals and possible outcomes that students are expected to demonstrate and achieve as a result.

What is the Web-Based Program Assessment System

Program assessment is defined as the systematic and ongoing method of gathering, analyzing and using information
from various sources about a program and measuring program outcomes in order to improve student learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Shown below are some of the relevant information about the Web-based Program Assessment System. You may contact us by sending us a message or feedback through our contact details.

  • Who can benefit from using the Web-Based Program Assessment System?

    Majority of the problems that the incoming first year students are facing today is what program they would like to take up in tertiary education, and the researchers believe that it is all up to their choices and decision itself. And in this way, the researchers can also analyze and identify this level through an assessment that may somehow help the students and their parents to address some issues concerning students’ interest. Making parents aware of the interests of their children towards their enrolled program may provide them basic information and better understanding through our proposed web-based program assessment system; as well as to be able to correct the misconceptions about the Department of Technology Education programs.

  • How to use the Web-Based Program Assessment System?

    The process consists of 3 basic steps:

    • Answer 10 questions based from 3 different specializations in Technology Education programs.
    • Select preferred skills and interests that are aligned to what specialization you are in.
    • View results according to what is your preferred major/specialization.
